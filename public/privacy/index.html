Friggo Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Friggo is a companion app for self-hosted Frigate NVR and Home Assistant installations. The app connects to infrastructure that you control and is designed to minimize data sharing.

Data We Handle

Friggo can process camera names, event labels, snapshots, notification payloads, and connection details in order to show your cameras, events, and alerts.

This data is primarily stored on your own Frigate, Home Assistant, and Firebase project infrastructure. We do not operate a separate commercial cloud service that stores your camera footage.

Notifications

Push notifications are delivered using your configured Firebase project and Apple/Google push services. Notification contents may include event labels, camera names, thumbnails, or snapshots depending on your settings.

Authentication

Friggo can authenticate with Home Assistant using its native OAuth flow and can register devices for push notifications. Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using platform secure storage.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for push delivery
  • Apple Push Notification service for iOS notification delivery
  • Google Play services for Android notification delivery where applicable

Open Source

Friggo and the associated Home Assistant integration are open source. Source code is available at github.com/Riddle-NEXT/FrigateMobile and github.com/Riddle-NEXT/frigate-notify-bridge.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/Riddle-NEXT/FrigateMobile/issues.
